About Us - Road Accident Detection

About Us

Our Mission

We aim to enhance road safety by utilizing the power of machine learning and modern technology. Our system is designed to detect live road accidents through CCTV cameras and immediately alert the appropriate emergency services. Where CCTV cameras are not available, our mobile camera section allows for video capture that our model analyzes to detect and respond to accidents promptly.

How It Works

Our advanced machine learning model monitors live feeds from CCTV cameras. If an accident is detected, an alert is sent to emergency services so they can take immediate action. In areas without CCTV coverage, users can capture video using their mobile devices, and our model will analyze the footage in real-time.

Why Choose Us?

Our solution offers a reliable and efficient way to monitor road safety, ensuring that help is dispatched as quickly as possible in the event of an accident. By leveraging both stationary and mobile cameras, we cover more ground and save lives by reducing response times.
